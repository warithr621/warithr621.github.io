Competitive Programming

08 January 2021 - Present

  • USACO:

    USACO is the primary programming contest in the United States, hosting four competitions annually and allowing the highest-ranked students to participate in a summer camp leading to IOI, the International Olympiad in Informatics. Below is the current list of all USACO contests I have participated in.

    1. 2021 January Contest on 22-25 January 2021: A score of 513/1000, placing 2718th out of 4173 pre-college bronze participants.
    2. 2021 December Contest on 17-20 December 2021: A score of 721/1000, placing 1458th out of 7673 pre-college bronze participants. In this contest, I scored high enough to be promoted to the Silver division.
    3. 2022 January Contest on 28-31 January 2022: A score of 142/1000, placing 1445th out of 2901 pre-college silver participants.
