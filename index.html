About Me

Hi, I'm Warith! I graduated from Allen High School in the Dallas area, and am now an incoming sophomore at UT Austin studying CS and Math. I created this website to help share a bit more about me, and additionally practice my own web design skills.

While in high school, some of the main activities I did included competing in the University Interscholastic League (winning two state golds in my senior year), teaching students through volunteering and work, and exploring new passions and hobbies. I also got into competitive programming throughout high school, and am trying to get back into it now through resources like Codeforces, Leetcode, and UTPC (UT Austin's Programming Club). Right now, I'm trying to dive into the field of cybersecurity as an intersection of my interests in puzzle-solving and math.

Outside of academics, I love to keep up with soccer and cricket, and often use my free time chatting with my friends or playing on TETR.IO. I also love listening to K-pop whether I'm studying for a test or out on a run, getting into the genre during high school (although I'm yet to visit a concert).
