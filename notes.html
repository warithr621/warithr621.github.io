School Notes :D

Since we can all agree school can get a bit confusing at times, notes are a helpful way to keep track of information and a refresher before starting an assignment or an important exam. I originally created a set of Pre-Calculus Notes in LaTeX through Overleaf in order to get ahead on this course, but then realized how helpful this could be to others. I decided to share this via Google Drive for others, before recognizing the fact that not everyone I know will necessarily be taking this course next year. I then started working on new notes, having just finished making Algebra II notes based on Allen ISD's Curriculum (embedded on the right. Hopefully, more and more of these notes will be made and utilized in the future!
